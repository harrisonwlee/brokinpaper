ABOUT
Born and raised in Queens, New York, Harrison Lee, a.k.a. BrokinPaper began his music career producing music in 2009. Since then, he has produced music across a variety of genres ranging from hip-hop/rap to more electronic-infused genres all in an effort to discover who he wanted BrokinPaper to eventually become. Finding influence in older, more "old-school" analog sounds, BrokinPaper's second, self-released EP titled THE MASLOW EP, has been compared to the works of artists like Gesaffelstein, SebastiAn, Boys Noize, Rezz, and Brodinski; using both elements from techno and house music as well as styles from hip hop/rap, reminiscent of his musical upbringing.
